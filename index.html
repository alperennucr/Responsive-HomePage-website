ABOUT US

VALOREX HOLDINGS INC. is a privately owned US based, well diversified investment holdings group that invests into various technology & service providers that add value to Next Generation Telecommunications & Communications space with over 20 years of expertise in related technologies and access to financial resources.

VALUE INVESTMENTS & PARTERNESHIPS
Carrier Services & Solutions
